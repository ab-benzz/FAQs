  • Ultra HD: Affordable, bright, and reliable.
  • OLED: Best overall picture quality with perfect blacks, vibrant colors, and slim design.
  • QLED: Uses quantum dots for richer colors and higher brightness—great for bright rooms.

OLED is generally considered the best for picture quality, though QLED and high-end LED are excellent alternatives.

  • 4K: 4x the detail of Full HD. 4K TVs offer a resolution of 3,840 x 2,160 pixels, resulting in sharp visuals, vibrant colors, and support for High Dynamic Range (HDR), which creates a more lifelike picture.

Modern 4K TVs use upscaling technology to enhance lower-resolution or non-4K content, making images clearer and more detailed.

  • 8K: 4x the detail of 4K. 8K TVs provide a resolution of 7,680 x 4,320 pixels, enhancing clarity even further.
  • Mini LED: Uses smaller, more precise LED backlights for enhanced brightness control and contrast. Ideal for bright rooms and detailed HDR performance.
  • QLED (Quantum Dot LED): Powered by quantum dots, QLED TVs deliver vivid colors, high brightness, and energy efficiency — great for well-lit spaces.
  • OLED (Organic LED): Each pixel lights up individually, resulting in perfect blacks, infinite contrast, and ultra-thin design. Best for cinematic viewing and dark rooms.
  • Nano: Utilizes nanoparticles to filter out color impurities, resulting in richer, more accurate colors and a wider viewing angle. Great for families or group viewing, where color consistency and clarity matter from every angle.

60Hz is fine for most shows and movies, but 120Hz is better for sports, gaming, and fast-action scenes for smoother motion.

A soundbar is a great choice if you want a simple, space-saving setup that still delivers a big upgrade over built-in TV speakers. If you prefer maximum audio quality, and a true home theater feel, go for separate speakers; they offer richer sound and scalability, though they require more space, cables like HDMI, and setup.

Modern TVs are energy-efficient. Look for an Energy Star Rating if you want to save electricity.

Commercial TVs are designed for businesses that need reliable screens running all day. They’re built to last with durable design, better cooling, and helpful features like easy content management, flexible mounting options, and security settings. Perfect for restaurants, hotels, or retail shops, these TVs make it simple to showcase menus, promotions, and information in a professional, eye-catching way.
